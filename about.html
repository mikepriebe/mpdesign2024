About

Hello!
I'm Mike Priebe

I have twice been the founding UX/UI designer at startups using Artificial Intelligence for good, both exited. I know how to get the important stuff done to launch successfully and have the experience and wisdom to scale.

I enjoy working at fast-paced startups designing user-centred design solutions for SaaS enterprise products serving global clients producing impactful results. I thrive on collaboration, utilizing the whole team to inform the design.

I love purpose driven missions, working with socially and environmentally-focused startup founders. I am optimistic, curious, relentless in tackling challenges, a visionary, and obsessed with untangling complexity. I solve complex problems with simple design and intuitive experiences.

I have utilized UX/UI principles and methods since 2011 in web design and product design.

I live in a suburb community of Vancouver near the ocean. When I'm not designing I'm busy being a Dad to two lovely girls, hiking mountains, creating board games, and playing music.

I love ❤ connecting and chatting about design. Let's grab a coffee, virtually or locally ☕
